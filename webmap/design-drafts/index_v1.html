PUTTING YOUTH ON THE MAP

An information resource for youth and adults working to ensure youth well-being in California.


Putting Youth On the Map Offers:

Data, tools and maps to help identify youth populations and places in greatest need of care and investment.
Measures across a range of important issues – including education, health, social relationships and community resources and engagement.
Information on how youth and adults can use these data to improve youth well-being.
