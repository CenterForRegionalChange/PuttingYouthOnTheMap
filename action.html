Stories

Sacramento

Sacramento youth leaders are putting youth on the map! This summer a team from South Sacramento Building Healthy Communities worked with UC Davis to help pilot this website. See here for a story and video about their effort, courtesy of AccessLocal.TV. Come back later to learn more about how youth are using data in their local organizing and advocacy.

Coachella

Coachella youth are Putting Youth on the Map! On October 3-4, 2014, youth from the Building Healthy Communities, Eastern Coachella Valley site, representing the Neighborhoods, Prevention and Schools Action Teams, along with young adults representing the Boards and Commissions team, all participated in a Putting Youth on the Map pilot workshop. Youth learned about the power of mapping, the content and functions of PYOM and how to use data and maps as part of their local change work.

Youth from the Action Teams will present what they learned to the adult allies on the Actions Teams, with a proposal to have youth play a larger role in action team work and put their knowledge of data and mapping to use. Stay tuned to learn more about the progress of this work.
